DealXanh is an O2O (Online-to-Offline) marketplace platform designed to tackle the widespread crisis of food waste in urban areas. By bridging the gap between food retailers (bakeries, convenience stores, and restaurants) and budget-conscious consumers, DealXanh facilitates the sale of quality, near-expiry food at highly discounted prices (up to 70% off).

Built on a robust two-sided marketplace architecture, the platform ensures secure online pre-payments and convenient offline in-store pickups, promoting a sustainable lifestyle and circular economy in the F&B industry.
